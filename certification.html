Green Productivity Specialists

A Summary of APO-GPS 201:2023 Certification Requirements - Developed by Leonard Tiopan Panjaitan, MT, CSRA, GPS - 2025

What is Green Productivity (GP)?

Green Productivity (GP) is a strategy for enhancing productivity and environmental performance for overall socioeconomic development. It's an integrated approach to achieving sustainable economic growth.

Job Responsibilities of a GP Specialist

  • ✓ Define the scope of project assignments and diagnose productivity and environmental issues.
  • ✓ Recommend and assist in planning, executing, and sustaining appropriate GP solutions.
  • ✓ Conduct research, promotion, and training related to GP concepts and techniques.
  • ✓ Facilitate GP solutions in an ethical, productive, and transparent manner.

Framework for Green Productivity Improvement

1. Getting Started

Team formation and walk-through survey for information collection.

2. Planning

Identification of problems and causes, and setting objectives and targets.

3. Generating & Evaluating Options

Generating, screening, and prioritizing various GP solution options.

4. Implementing GP Options

Formulating plans, implementing selected options, and building competence.

5. Monitoring & Reviewing

Monitoring and evaluating results, and conducting management reviews.

6. Sustaining GP

Incorporating changes into systems and identifying areas for continuous improvement.

Levels of Certification

Green Productivity Specialist (Tier 1)

The foundational level for professionals meeting the initial requirements.

  • Qualification: University degree/diploma + 5 years experience, or equivalent.
  • Knowledge: Completed CGPS Preparatory Course & passed written exam.
  • Experience: At least 1 GP assignment (min. 360 work hours) in the last 12 months.
  • Testimonial: At least 1 positive testimonial from a client.

Master Green Productivity Specialist (Tier 2)

The advanced level for experienced and proven specialists.

  • Qualification: Must be a certified GP Specialist (Tier 1).
  • Experience: Carried out at least 5 GP assignments (consulting, promotion, training, or research).
  • Testimonial: Submitted at least 5 positive testimonials from clients.

Core Competencies

GP Domain Expertise

GP methodology, 3Rs, LCA, TQM, Eco-efficiency, Lean & Green, Green Supply Chain, etc.

Process Skills

Consultancy, Training, Promotion, and Applied Research related to GP.

People Skills

Client Relationship, Stakeholder Management, Communication, and Team Management.
