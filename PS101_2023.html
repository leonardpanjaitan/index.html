What the Scheme Is

Goal

Build region-wide pools of Certified Productivity Specialists (CPS) who can plan, execute, and sustain productivity improvement for clients.

Who runs it?

APO (scheme owner) via accredited Certification Bodies (CBs); NPOs and other entities may act as CBs.

Recognition

  • Internationally-recognized credential
  • Listing in CB public registers
  • Use of certification logos
  • Access to APO networks and assignments

Value Snapshot

  • Stronger career & business prospects
  • Standardized competency benchmark
  • Proof of commitment to CPD
  • Network & visibility across APO economies

Scope & Services

Certified persons deliver: Consultancy, Training, Promotion, and Research to implement appropriate productivity solutions for client organizations.

Key Outcomes: Measurable productivity gains, strengthened internal capability, and sustained improvement.
