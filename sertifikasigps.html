Green Productivity Specialists

Certification Process based on APO-GPS 201:2023

1

Application

Submit the application form along with required documents: CV, project reports, and training certificates.

Document Review

The Certification Body (CB) reviews the submitted documents for completeness and compliance.

2
3

Examination

Candidates must pass a written examination to test their knowledge of Green Productivity.

Interview

A panel of experts interviews the candidate to assess their practical skills and experience.

4
5

Approval & Certification

Issuance of the certificate, valid for a 3-year period.
